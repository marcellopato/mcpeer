Logo Concept

The MCPeer logo represents the core concept of the Model Context Protocol - interconnected systems working together seamlessly. The central hub (yellow circle) symbolizes the MCPeer platform, while the surrounding nodes (gray circles) represent different APIs, services, and integrations that can be connected through MCP.

The connecting lines show the flow of data and context between systems, emphasizing MCPeer's role as the visual interface that makes these complex integrations simple and accessible to developers and teams.
